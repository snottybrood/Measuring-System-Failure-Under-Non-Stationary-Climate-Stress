# Method Card (Plain Language)

## What this method answers
1) How is severe rainfall frequency changing?
2) Where is it changing?
3) Who is repeatedly exposed to flood risk?

## Inputs
- PRISM precipitation (observed)
- FEMA flood hazard layers (context)
- USGS stream gauges (context)
- Census/ACS vulnerability indicators

## Outputs
- Frequency maps
- Change maps (epoch comparisons)
- Vulnerability overlays
- Summary tables for planning
